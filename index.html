Nenenna Nwakanma

Nnenna Nwakanma was born 1975 in south-eastern Nigeria. Nnenna is the co-founder of Free Software, Open Source Foundation, The Africa Network of Information Society Actors, and the African Civil Society for Africa. She is also a former member of the board of Open Source Initiative. And she previously served as the Information Officer for the Helen Keller foundation in Africa.
